Why Inferploy?

INFERPLOY is going to connect startups all over india exclusively to high school students. We are planning to help both students and startups to grow in this process with our meticulously designed curriculum, solving both major problems in the startup industry as well as the education industry simultaneously. INFERPLOY in its initial stages is going to operate through an easily accessible website where all students can openly check out all startups and apply as well as startups can apply to be a part of our community.
